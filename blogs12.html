Let’s start by understanding the need for a Shopify store audit.

Why does a Shopify store audit matter?

Understanding your Shopify performance is crucial for growing your online business. It helps you identify bottlenecks and areas for optimal performance. Thus, you can utilize these audit insights to enhance your Shopify store, improve the user experience, and increase conversions.

  • The performance barriers that lead to a higher bounce rate

So, what can a Shopify audit reveal about your store? The free Shopify audit checklist!

Our free Shopify store audit focuses on the three core KPI categories: User Interface, User Experience, and Performance. You can use the following checklist to self-audit or claim our free Shopify store audit for hassle-free analysis!

User Interface (UI) Audit

'First impression is the last’

94% of users form their first impression of a store based on its design or user interface ( resource ). That’s why the first step in our free audit is to check your Shopify store’s user interface.

Theme compatibility

The theme is the heart of any Shopify store. The theme isn’t just about the colour and design, but also its compatibility with other browsers and devices. Therefore, our experts verify the compatibility of the theme based on its performance across various devices and browsers.

Furthermore, we check if the design layout aligns with your industry. This is because Shopify has hundreds of themes, and not all of them meet the needs of your niche. The wrong theme can slow down the speed and mislead your visitors. Therefore, Shopify theme compatibility matters. 

Page layout based on visual hierarchy 

You only have a few seconds (less than 3 seconds) to grab your target audience’s attention when they open your store’s website. Therefore, understanding visual hierarchy is critical to creating a captivating Shopify page layout. 

A visual hierarchy helps you create a layout that attracts users’ attention and reduces their cognitive load. Here, we focus on your Shopify store’s menu bar, navigation, hero banner, CTAs, and text & image placement. 

The more strategic your page layout, the greater the chances are that users will spend more time on your website. 

Branding assessment

Branding isn’t just about the logo; it also includes the colour and typography used to stimulate certain emotions. We analyze the placement and impact of your visual elements, like the logo, icons, colours, typography, and content voice. We can help you optimize it based on competitive research and industry standards to maximize the ROI on branding. 

User Experience (UX) Audit

As per a source, 32% of users abandon their favourite brands if they have had one bad experience. So, imagine the situation if a first-time visitor has a bad user experience; they are likely never to come back. Furthermore, your brand image may suffer if you experience multiple such incidents. 

Therefore, our free UX audit focuses on analyzing your store’s visual and functional elements that affect your conversion. 

Navigation assessment 

The navigation bar is your store’s lifeline, as most customers use the navigation menu to find products or the necessary information.

However, many Shopify stores fail to accommodate their product categories in the navigation bar due to poor planning. In such cases, their users may not find what they are looking for and leave the website, resulting in fewer conversions. 

Many Shopify store developers rely on creating horizontal tabs to list their products. However, research shows that 27% of users don’t bother looking at hidden content. All your product and informative pages must be accessible in 3-4 clicks. 

We analyze the effectiveness of your store’s navigation and offer alternative options to improve it. Contact us now for a free Shopify audit of your store’s navigation system.

Audit of page elements (content, media, sorting, and more)

Next, we analyze the functionality of your store’s page elements. Here, our team checks if your content and media are readable. We provide you with suggestions on how to make your page layout intuitive and action-driven.
